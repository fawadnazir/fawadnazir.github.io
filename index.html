About Me

I have over fifteen (15) years of experience in the technology industry. I am a passionate leader focused on building world-class teams, exceptional customer experienceand have a strong track record of delivering valuable outcomes. I always help my teams understand the critical importance of putting the customer at the center of everything they do and delivering outcomes that exceed expectations. I have held several key roles across multiple organizations covering Product Management, Product Innovation & Marketing, Sales, Business Development, Enterprise Architecture, Program Management, Business Strategy and Transformation, Research and Development, major multi-million Digital Transformations, and Customer Innovation roles. I have worked with several customers in a wide range of industries including mining, healthcare, education, banking, and financial services, retail, construction, airlines, government, emergency services, and more.

I enjoy leading teams to solve challenging problems. I have worked on some of the world’s most complex challenges. For example, my published and patented work on discovering the topology of large and complex networks within seconds at CERN Switzerland, my work on analyzing and visualizing large datasets coming from the world largest linear particle accelerators at Stanford University in the US, and writing algorithms to execute algorithmic trading arbitrage opportunity in nanoseconds.

My specific expertise is in the field of product development, product design, sales leadership, product marketing, business development, business transformation, customer success, value differentiation, and innovation, including idea generation, solution delivery, and human-centered design thinking.

I received my education at the University of New South Wales Australia with a Doctorate Degree in Electrical Engineering. I completed my post-graduation in research at Stanford University in the US. I also hold a bachelor's in computer science from the National University of Science and Technology, Pakistan.

I live in Sydney with my wife and three children. I enjoy running and continue my great passion for golf.
